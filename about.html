Who We Are

Lotos Charitable Trust is a small group of passionate individuals dedicated to helping people in every possible way. Our activities include planting trees, organizing blood donation drives, distributing sanitary pads in small villages, and providing food to those in need.

Our Vision

We believe that even small efforts can lead to big change. Our vision is to create a more equal, healthy, and caring society where no one is left behind.

Our Mission

To reach every corner where help is needed — whether it’s environmental support, healthcare, or education.
